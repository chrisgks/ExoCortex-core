# Scope

- `context_path`: `.`
- `scope`: `root`
- `owner_agent`: `knowledge-steward`
- `parent_wiki`: none
- `child_wikis`: none yet
- `promotion_rule`: keep knowledge local by default and promote into the root wiki only when it becomes reusable
  across contexts inside ExoCortex

## Purpose

This is the root managed wiki for ExoCortex. It should hold ExoCortex-wide architecture, policy, operating
knowledge, and links to narrower child wikis when they exist.
