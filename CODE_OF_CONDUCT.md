# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Treat other contributors with respect. Prefer direct, technical, evidence-backed disagreement over personal attacks, mockery, or status games.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Deliberate publication of private or identifying information without consent
- Bad-faith collaboration, including knowingly hiding security, privacy, or data-loss risks

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this standard.
