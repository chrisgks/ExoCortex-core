# Synthesis Studio

The generic slot for pulling scattered work into one place and producing
proposals from it. Adapt the phases to your own material.

## When to use

- You have substantial work spread across several projects, repos, or notes.
- The goal is a consolidated plan or proposal, not a one-off summary.

## When not to use

- A single-source question → answer it inline.
- The work already lives in one place.

## Steps (adapt to your own process)

1. **Inventory** — list where the relevant work lives; read the sources.
2. **Cluster** — group what recurs across sources by theme, not by where it came from.
3. **Select** — pick a few concrete options from the gaps between clusters.
4. **Propose** — write up each option as a short plan, tuned to its audience.
5. **Record** — promote durable findings back into your knowledge base.

## Guardrails

- Human-in-loop: propose, don't decide.
- Cite where each finding came from.
