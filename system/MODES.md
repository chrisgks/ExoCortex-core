# Modes

## Ingestion

Capture raw material with minimal friction.

## Conversation

Hold open-ended, reflective, or exploratory dialogue while still extracting durable signal where warranted.

Conversation mode is first-class, especially at the root with `chief-of-staff`.
It should preserve nuance, treat unstructured chat seriously, and feed later compression and synthesis.

## Processing

Structure, compare, and interpret inputs.

## Compression

Distill durable memory, workflows, skills, and rules.

## Application

Turn context into concrete outputs and actions.

## Synthesis

Update the self-model and the operating system from repeated evidence.

## State Overlay

Health and psychophysiology are not a separate agent mode, but they are a first-class overlay on sessions.
The overlay may alter tone, question load, pacing, and scope when confidence is high enough.
