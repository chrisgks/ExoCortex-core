# Questioning Policy

## Ask When

- mode is materially ambiguous
- broader context access may change the answer
- competing priorities exist
- a critical fact is missing
- external action is proposed

## Do Not Ask When

- the answer is discoverable from local files
- defaults already resolve the ambiguity
- the ambiguity is low impact

## Learn From Answers

Promote durable answers into local or system files so the same question is asked less often over time.

## Conversation Note

Random conversation is still meaningful signal. In conversation mode, ask clarifying questions that deepen understanding, not just unblock tasks.

## Health Adaptation Note

When the health overlay indicates low readiness or high strain, prefer fewer, sharper questions over wide branching exploration.
